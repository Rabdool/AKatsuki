About Itachi

Itachi Uchiha was a prodigy of the Uchiha clan, known for his brilliance and power from a young age. He joined the Akatsuki after tragically annihilating his own clan, a mission he accepted to prevent a civil war. Despite his dark reputation, Itachi's actions were driven by love for his younger brother Sasuke and a desire for peace.
