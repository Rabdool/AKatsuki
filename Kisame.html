About Kisame

Known for his monstrous sword, Samehada, Kisame was feared for his ability to absorb chakra and unleash devastating attacks in combat. He formed a partnership with Itachi Uchiha within the Akatsuki, and they were tasked with hunting down the Tailed Beasts.
