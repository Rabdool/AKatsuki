About Zetsu

Zetsu is one of the most enigmatic members of the Akatsuki, known for his dual personality and his unique appearance. He is a combination of two personalities: Black Zetsu and White Zetsu. Black Zetsu is a manifestation of Kaguya Ootsutsuki's will and has a complex agenda involving the manipulation of key events in the story. White Zetsu, on the other hand, is a plant-based organism with the ability to regenerate and is known for being highly skilled in tracking and spying. Zetsu is often seen as a scout or intelligence gatherer for the organization.
